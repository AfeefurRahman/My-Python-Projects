\f0\b\fs26 \ul \uc0\u55357 \u56481  Inspiration:
\f1\b0\fs24 \ulnone \
\
Moving to a new country is overwhelming. With over 8,000 international students at UB, many struggle with "unwritten rules"\'97from dining options and campus slang to mental health resources and tipping culture.\
\
UB GlobalPal solves this by providing an always-available, non-judgmental AI friend that doesn't just translate languages\'97it translates culture. It's pre-trained with specific local knowledge about Buffalo and UB campus life.\
\
